This curriculum supports classroom and laboratory use of **Avida-ED** to explore
core concepts in evolution using evolving digital organisms.

## Curriculum components

- **Quick Start**  
  A short pathway to get students working with Avida-ED quickly.

- **Lab Manual**  
  A sequence of 4–5 guided exercises, plus an independent research project.

- **Model Lessons**  
  Classroom-ready lessons focused on specific evolutionary concepts.

## Recommended starting point
New people should begin with **Quick Start**, then proceed to **Lab Manual – Exercise 1**.
