and institutions working to improve evolution education.

## Project character

This history matters because Avida-ED was built as a joint education and
research effort rather than as a classroom app produced in isolation. The site
should continue to reflect that it draws on:

- project leadership and contributors
- institutional affiliations
- funding and acknowledgment information
- historical context for how Avida-ED developed over time

## Acknowledgment role

This page is the place to preserve project credits and institutional context so
that student, instructor, and support pages can stay focused on classroom use.

As additional legacy material is migrated, this page should expand to include a
cleaner contributor timeline and any funding acknowledgments that need to stay
visible in the public site.
